# NAFLD Multi-Omics Dataset Candidates — Reference Notes

## Spatial Transcriptomics

### Option 1: Li et al. 2025 (Ideal, access-restricted)
- Paper: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-025-02407-8
- Journal: Nature Genetics, 2025
- Disease: MASLD/NAFLD-specific (control, MASL, MASH)
- Sample size: 61 patients, 540,216 cells, 47,864 spatial spots
- Technology: 10x Visium (spatial transcriptomics) + spatial metabolomics (MALDI-MSI)
- Key finding: TREM2+ lipid-associated macrophages (LAMs)
- Access: Controlled access required — NGDC OMIX010136, contact jin.chai@cldcsw.org
- Access request: https://ngdc.cncb.ac.cn/omix/request/OMIX010136
- Status: Not currently accessible without approval

### Option 2: Watson et al. 2025 (Accessible now)
- Paper: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-024-55325-4
- Journal: Nature Communications, 2025
- Disease: General liver fibrosis (not confirmed NAFLD-specific)
- Sample size: 3 healthy + 3 fibrotic donors; ~90,000 cells (healthy) + ~220,000 cells (fibrotic)
- Technology: MERFISH (317-gene targeted panel) + snRNA-seq
- Important caveat: Paper explicitly states SPP1/GPNMB do NOT show expected 
  lipid-associated macrophage pattern in this dataset
- Access: Public, CC0 license, immediately downloadable
- Data link: https://datadryad.org/dataset/doi:10.5061/dryad.37pvmcvsg
- File size: ~2.23 GB (main processed file)

## Metabolomics

### Option 1: Ji et al. 2022 (Directly verified)
- Paper: https://www.mdpi.com/2227-9059/10/7/1669
- Journal: Biomedicines, 2022
- Sample size: 86 subjects (25 healthy, 42 NAFL, 19 NASH)
- Molecules: 79 metabolites (amino acids, organic acids, fatty acids, nucleosides)
- Sample type: Plasma (blood)
- Staging: Broad categories only (Healthy/NAFL/NASH), no F0-F4
- Access: Open access, CC BY 3.0, confirmed downloadable

### Option 2: McGlinchey et al. 2022 (Real, data location unverified)
- Paper DOI: 10.1016/j.jhepr.2022.100477
- Journal: JHEP Reports, 2022 (EASL official journal)
- Sample size: 627 patients — European NAFLD Registry
- Staging: Full fibrosis staging F0-F4, key finding at F2-F3 transition
- Molecules: Lipids and polar metabolites
- Access: CC BY 4.0, open access — exact data file location not yet confirmed
